BlockDrop is a shameless Tetris® clone using pure JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I started writing it while teaching myself how to use JavaScript as a proper language and wean myself off jQuery. I'm also using it as a testing platform for various JavaScript, HTML5 and CSS3 features. I've tested in Chrome, Firefox, IE 9/10 and Chrome for Android so it should work on most modern platforms.

Controls:

a h or swipe
moves the piece left

d l or swipe
moves the piece right

w k swipe , or single tap
rotates the piece 90° clock-wise

s j or swipe
soft drops the piece (drop line by line)

spacebar or ↵ enter
hard drops the piece (drop all the way to the bottom)
